# AI头像生成Prompt

## 最终确定方案

**风格**: 2.5D半写实风格（介于二次元和真实照片之间）

**主体描述**:
A young Asian male developer, 25-27 years old, clean and modern appearance, wearing casual tech style clothing (simple t-shirt or hoodie), short neat hair, optional thin-framed glasses, natural friendly smile or thoughtful expression, warm lighting, soft focus background suggesting a modern workspace or coffee shop, professional but approachable vibe, high quality digital art, semi-realistic anime style (2.5D), trending on ArtStation

**中文版**:
一个年轻的亚洲男性开发者，25-27岁，干净现代的外表，穿着休闲科技风格的衣服（简单的T恤或卫衣），短发整洁，可选细框眼镜，自然友好的微笑或思考的表情，温暖的光线，柔焦背景暗示现代工作空间或咖啡店，专业但平易近人的氛围，高质量数字艺术，半写实动漫风格（2.5D），ArtStation热门风格

**负面提示词 (Negative Prompt)**:
ugly, blurry, low quality, distorted face, anime eyes too large, overly cartoonish, suit and tie, formal wear, messy hair, unkempt, dark lighting, cluttered background, photorealistic, 3D render, corporate headshot

**推荐生成工具**:
- Midjourney: `/imagine prompt: [上述prompt] --ar 1:1 --style raw --v 6`
- DALL-E 3: 直接使用上述prompt
- Stable Diffusion: 使用上述prompt + 负面提示词，推荐模型：Anything V5 或 RealisticVision

**备选方案（如果2.5D效果不理想）**:

方案B - 简约插画风:
```
Minimalist illustration of a young male developer, simple line art, clean geometric shapes, wearing glasses and headphones, warm color palette (orange, teal, cream), flat design, modern tech aesthetic, icon style, professional avatar
```

方案C - 像素风:
```
Pixel art avatar of a young developer, 64x64 style upscaled, retro gaming aesthetic, wearing casual clothes and glasses, warm colors, clean background, nostalgic but modern
```

## 使用说明

1. 优先使用"最终确定方案"生成
2. 生成3-5个变体，选择最符合"阳光、干净、有点技术宅但不油腻"气质的
3. 头像尺寸建议：
   - 小红书：800x800px
   - X: 400x400px
4. 确保人物表情自然，不要过度修饰
5. 背景要简洁，不抢主体

## 色调参考

- 主色调：暖色系（米色、浅橙、暖灰）
- 避免：冷硬的蓝紫色、过于鲜艳的颜色
- 光线：柔和自然光，避免强烈阴影
